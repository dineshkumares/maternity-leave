Maternity Leave IN WEEKS

Currently, the United States is the only developed nation with no federal policy for maternity leave. As a result, new mothers must rely on the state government and their employers to grant them maternity leave and the benefits surrounding maternity leave.

Netflix and the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation are among some of the companies with the longest maternity leaves, coming in at 52 weeks.
