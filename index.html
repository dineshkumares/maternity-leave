Maternity Leave IN WEEKS

Currently, the United States is the only developed nation with no federal policy for paid maternity leave.

Sweden, Estonia, Iceland, Lithuania, Slovenia, Hungary, Norway, and Finland offer some of the best federally mandated paid maternity leaves in the world.

As a result, new mothers must rely on the state government and their employers to grant them paid maternity leave and the benefits surrounding maternity leave. This data is made available by FairyGoodBoss, a online platform for sharing reviews about companies by women, for women.

Netflix and the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation are among some of the companies with the longest maternity leaves, coming in at 52 weeks.

When we sort by industry, we see that the largest circles are contained within the Technology sector.
