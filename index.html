Maternity Leave IN WEEKS

Currently, the United States is the only developed nation that has no federal policy for paid maternity leave.

Which developed countries have federally mandated maternity leave policies?

Sweden, Estonia, Iceland, Lithuania, Slovenia, Hungary, Norway, and Finland offer some of the best federally mandated paid maternity leaves in the world.

New mothers must rely on their employers

As a result, many new mothers must rely mainly on their employers to grant them paid maternity leave and benefits.

This bubble chart visualizes maternity leave in weeks for various companies and educational institutions

This data is made available by FairyGoodBoss, a online platform for sharing reviews about companies by women, for women.

Netflix and the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation are among some of the companies with the longest maternity leaves, coming in at 52 weeks.

On the higher end of the spectrum, we see companies offering 30 weeks of paid maternity leave or more.

On the lower end of the spectrum, some companies, like Starbucks, offer 6 weeks of paid maternity leave. This number also varies depending on the type of employee - for example, baristas at Starbucks are offered a shorter maternity leave than employees in the corporate office.

The average maternity leave duration for reported employers is 21.2 weeks, according to the dataset provided by FairyGoodBoss.
